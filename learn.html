Our Galaxy: The Milky Way

 Our galaxy, the Milky Way Galaxy, has an estimated 100 billion planets. In our solar system, which is also known as the Copernican, Heliocentric, or Planetary System, we have the planets Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Our dwarf planets include Pluto, Eris, Makemake, Haumea, and Ceres.


Our solar system is located within one of the outer spirals of the Milky Way called the Orion Arm.
